ABOUT THE APP

Welcome to the New Companies Act(NCA), 2013 Android App.

The Companies Act, 2013 is now fully in force from 1st April, 2014. This android version of the New Companies Act, 2013 "NCA, 2013" has the following features:

  • Works completely offline at your convenience
  • Includes All Sections, Rules and Schedules
  • Easy navigation between Chapters
  • Search between Sections, Rules or Schedules
  • Chapter wise Forms (coming soon)
  • Notification and Circulars (coming soon)
  • Realtime notifications (coming soon)
  • Updates every month

NCA, 2013 is a compendium for all CORPORATE PROFESSIONALS.
